# Code of Conduct

We want `ssyubix` to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming open source
project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent, but be clear about technical concerns
- Help new contributors get oriented
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on identity or background
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith participation
- Sharing private information without permission

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, or pull requests that
do not follow this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to
temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
privately through GitHub. Public issues should be used for technical
discussion, not for handling personal safety concerns.
